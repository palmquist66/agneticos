I highly recommend Cary Grove Animal Hospital. After five years of taking cats to different vets in the city and suburbs, this was my first time at Cary Grove and also my first time with a dog. I took my 9-week-old Bloodhound, Watson, to get checked out due to a skin condition and will definitely continue to go back. From the front desk staff, to the vet tech, Shauna, and vet, Dr. Stovall, everyone was friendly, thorough, and most importantly they were all caring. I've been to several pet stores, vet clinics, groomers, etc where it's pretty obvious that the employees don't have their heart in their work, but the staff here went above and beyond. They even went an hour past their typical closing time due to them wanting to ensure they could do all the testing that Dr. Stovall recommended. In addition, they were upfront and transparent about all estimated costs. Many animal hospitals are happy to help but then surprise you with a larger-than-expected bill at the very end, but they were really honest. And when the vet recommended two new things, they redid the estimate with the additional costs.



But what was most appreciated was how empathetic they were toward both my puppy and myself. Although they had to do a minor skin treatment that was pretty painful for Watson, they were all as gentle as they could be and did everything they could to keep him distracted and calm. They definitely know how to spoil their patients. I think all pet owners have seen groomers and technicians at places like big box stores who handle the pets a little too roughly, but they were great here. And 2-3 times they came to the room where I was waiting to give me updates and reassure me that he was okay.

I am beyond grateful to Dr. Alison Baker, Vet Tech Colette, Vet Tech Sarah, and their front desk staff, made up of three of the most amazing receptionists EVER--April, Christine, and Colleen--as well as the dedicated team behind the scenes at Cary Grove Animal Hospital. Their exceptional care for my beloved dog, Sparky, has been nothing short of a miracle.



The journey started on 8/5 with a minor IVDD flare-up that was being treated with medications and laser therapy. But things took a critical turn on Sunday, August 10, when Sparky experienced severe gastrointestinal complications after being prescribed medication for his IVDD on 8/5. This led to a terrifying episode of gastrointestinal bleeding (HGE), low blood pressure, very low sodium and calcium levels, and severe dehydration, necessitating hospitalization for IV fluids and multiple tests. After spending $1,144.00 at Thrive Emergency Vet the previous night, I found myself financially unable to continue the care he desperately needed. Reluctantly, I brought him home against medical advice, knowing he may not make it through the night. But I prayed my heart out and hoped that if he could live through the night, Dr. Baker could save him because he required at least one or two days of intensive care and monitoring.



Despite the dire situation, Sparky showed incredible grit and an amazing will to live. I reached out to Christine at Cary Grove first thing Monday morning to explain his condition and how critical he was, and was met with immediate reassurance. She said, "Hold on one moment, let me tell Dr. Baker." Just two minutes later, she returned with the urgency I needed, exclaiming, "Come NOW!" When I arrived, I was greeted by the warm smiles of Christine and April, as well as that of Tech Sarah, eagerly waiting for her "munchkin."



Dr. Baker quickly assessed Sparky and meticulously reviewed his records from Thrive, formulating a comprehensive treatment plan and immediately getting to work. The staff treated him throughout Monday with fluids, antibiotics, pain medications, and antiemetics. I picked him up that night to bring him back the next day for continued care.



Throughout this entire process, the team made Sparky feel as if he were at home. They allowed him to snuggle into every available blanket they had because "he's needy" and even cozy up in laps for some well-deserved rocking, soothing, and spoiling. Their compassion extended beyond physical care; they kept me at ease with frequent updates, let me cry (like Lucy), and patiently answered every single question I had--believe me, there were a lot!



I am thrilled to share that Mr. Sparky is now well on his way to recovery! It's going to be a long and arduous journey, but with Cary Grove Animal Hospital by our side, there is no mountain I am not willing to face--except for one; and thanks to Dr. Baker's quick action, we won't have to address that dark day anytime soon!



The dedication and love from everyone at Cary Grove astonished not only me but also the charitable foundations I consulted to help with Sparky's care. Having worked with numerous veterinary offices, these organizations were blown away by the incredible level of care and compassion that Cary Grove provides.



If you are looking for a veterinary team that not only excels in expertise but also embodies genuine love for your pets, look no further than Cary Grove Animal Hospital. They have set the standard for what veterinary care should be, and I will always stand by this remarkable team. Thank you, Cary Grove, for everything! You are truly a blessing to our community!
