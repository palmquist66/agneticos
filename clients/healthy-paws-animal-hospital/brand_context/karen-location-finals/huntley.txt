With a population growth from 6,000 in the year 2000 to nearly 29,000 in 2026, it is no wonder that in one generation Huntley has gone from a few shops to a complete community.

What veterinary services do you offer Huntley pets?

We provide comprehensive care for dogs and cats including wellness exams, vaccinations, dental care, surgery, spay and neuter, senior pet support, nutrition counseling, and low-stress care for anxious animals.

What is your approach to fearful or anxious pets?

Your pet goes straight to an exam room when you arrive, no shared waiting area. We use gentle handling, calming techniques, and a pace that lets your pet settle before the exam begins. Many clients tell us their once-anxious pets seem calm pulling into our parking lot.

Do you see urgent cases for Huntley pet owners?

We handle urgent concerns during our regular business hours. For after-hours emergencies, we are happy to guide you to the nearest emergency facility. We recommend calling us first so we can advise on the best next step for your pet.

How do I schedule a first appointment?

Call us at (815) 322-5400. We will find a time that works for you and your pet, and our team will walk you through what to expect at your first visit.

Huntley Happenings for Cats and Dogs

Huntley is a community that loves its animals! A few local spots and events worth knowing:

Bark Park

One of the nicest dog parks in McHenry County. Located adjacent to Sunset Park in Lake in the Hills proper. It is a well maintained park whose land is divided, one side for large breed dogs the other side for our small breed friends.

Deicke Park

A jewel of a park with seemingly unlimited options to occupy your time. It is located off 47 south of Main Street. Dogs must be on leash, no longer than 8’ in length, when at the park.

Pet-Friendly Patios

After your visit, bring your dog along for a bite. Huntley Dairy Mart, Tropical Smoothie Cafe, and Culver's all offer outdoor seating where your pup is welcome. Sometimes the best part of a vet visit is the ice cream cone that follows.

Sit. Stay. Sip. — June 20, 2026

A fundraiser supporting senior pet adoptions, held at Sew Hop'd Brewery right here in Huntley. Live music, raffle baskets, and a great reason to get out with your fellow pet lovers.

Pawfest — October 3, 2026

An annual community celebration of pets at Deicke Park, hosted by the Huntley Park District. A fun-filled afternoon for the whole family and their four-legged members.
