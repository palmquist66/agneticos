- The clearest new-patient signal is the appointment requests, about 15 a month.

That's fine, it builds your reputation online, but it means the real local audience is smaller than the raw numbers suggest. The upside: that makes it very winnable. We don't need to reach everyone, just more of the local pet owners actually searching for a vet.

So this is our starting line, and the goal is simple: grow the calls and appointment requests by getting Healthy Paws in front of more local pet owners. I'll keep an eye on it and send you a short update as things move.
