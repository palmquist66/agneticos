## About Us

[Healthy Paws Animal Hospital](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/) is a high-quality, full-service, state-of-the-art veterinary practice that strives to exceed every client’s expectations. Our professional and courteous [staff](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/veterinarian-lake-in-the-hills-il/) provides the best possible medical, surgical, dental, and behavioral care for clients and their cherished pets. Our commitment is to provide an exceptional veterinary experience; communication and information are our cornerstones.

Services available include general wellness exams, senior wellness care, behavioral recommendations, nutrition counseling, general and emergency surgery, spaying/neutering, pain management, compassionate euthanasia services, digital dental radiography, and teeth cleaning. Regarding vaccination, Healthy Paws embraces “one size does not fit all” and customizes protocols to each patient, following the most recent and conservative guidelines.

![a dog looking at a stethoscope](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/1-1.jpg)

Our hospital has an onsite laboratory, isolation ward, modern surgery suite, and comprehensive pharmacy. Features such as convenient “in-room” checkout, floor-to-ceiling exam room windows, and paperless medical records set [Healthy Paws Animal Hospital](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/) apart while providing a more relaxing, streamlined experience for pets and their owners. Details are our specialty.

We are conveniently located on Lakewood Road, just south of Ackman Road, in Lake in the Hills, near Crystal Lake, Huntley, Village of Lakewood, and Algonquin. [Contact us](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/contact/) today to learn more about our veterinary practice and how we can serve the needs of you and your beloved pet.

## Our Mission

Healthy Paws provides medically advanced, comprehensive, and compassionate [veterinary care](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/lake-in-the-hills-il-veterinary-services/) in a timely and honest manner while promoting overall animal health and awareness for our clients and the community we serve.
