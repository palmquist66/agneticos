# Important Update:

**To ensure the highest level of patient data accuracy, we have upgraded our prescription protocols.** **Beginning April 1, 2026, your options for medication will be:**

- **In-Hospital:** Filled on-site.

- **Home Delivery:** Processed through our cost-competitive integrated online pharmacy for seamless home delivery.

- **Physical Script:** A paper script prepared for pickup (please allow 2 business days for preparation).

As pharmacies have moved to an integrated platform for human medicine, we have seen a significant increase in errors with faxed prescriptions. Upgrading our prescription protocols will allow us to maintain a seamless medical record for your pet and ensure their medications are handled with the direct oversight they deserve.

Thank you for partnering with us in providing the best care for your pets.
