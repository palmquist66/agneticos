# Pricing Transparency Canine

Thank you for your interest in Healthy Paws Animal Hospital and the services we provide. As a practice **_we focus on being personal, professional, and educational_**. Transparency in what we do and how we do it allows us to provide medical options to clients that are individualized to their pet’s and family’s needs.

Below we have listed pricing for vaccines and laboratory work commonly discussed during our canine comprehensive wellness exams. Medical care for your pet is never one-size-fits-all. **_Every pet is unique, and so is every treatment plan_**. We carefully consider options, benefits, risks, and details, then work with you to create a plan tailored specifically to your pet’s needs. At every visit we spend one-on-one time discussing your pet’s medical, behavioral, environmental, and social history allowing us to make individualized recommendations.

**Important Information About Vaccine Scheduling**

- Vaccine eligibility depends on your pet’s previous vaccination history and timing.
- Some vaccines require an initial booster series.
- If a vaccine is overdue, a booster may be required to ensure proper immunity.
- Final recommendations are based on your pet’s medical history, risk factors, and current guidelines.
- Your veterinarian will review your pet’s records and discuss the appropriate schedule for your individual dog.

**Laboratory Work** We recommend lab work based on your pet’s age, health status, lifestyle, and any current concerns. Testing options vary, with basic screening starting at approximately $101.60.
