## Pet End of Life Care

We understand that one of the most difficult decisions a pet owner may face is considering end-of-life care for a beloved companion. As your veterinary family, we are here to support you with compassion, respect, and understanding during this emotional time.

We offer in-office euthanasia services in a quiet, private space, ensuring a peaceful, pain-free passing for your pet. We approach every step of this process with compassion, ensuring that your pet is treated with the dignity and love they deserve.

### What to Expect

- A gentle sedative is given first, so your pet is calm and comfortable.
- You are welcome to stay with your pet throughout, or step away if that feels best.
- We offer time and space for private goodbyes.
- Aftercare options are available, including cremation and memorial keepsakes.

We understand the difficulty of this decision. If you have any questions or would like to discuss next steps, please don’t hesitate to reach out. Our team is here to help, without pressure or judgment.

### End of Life At-Home Options

We understand how difficult it can be to say goodbye to a beloved companion. For families who prefer to have this done in the comfort of their home, there are at-home euthanasia options available. Please contact our team for more information and guidance about these services. We’re here to support you and help ensure your pet’s passing is peaceful and respectful.
