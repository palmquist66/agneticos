# Meet Our Veterinary Team

We hope to see you soon! Discover [Healthy Paws Animal Hospital’s](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/about-healthy-paws-animal-hospital/) fantastic team and why we love what we do.

## Our team is, hands down, what makes us most different.

Our veterinary team at [Healthy Paws Animal Hospital](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/about-healthy-paws-animal-hospital/) embodies a compassionate and dedicated group of professionals committed to the well-being of your furry companions. With a wealth of expertise and a genuine love for animals, each team member plays a crucial role in providing quality care for pets. We foster a warm and welcoming environment, recognizing the importance of building trust and rapport with both pets and their owners.

## Our Cats

![clinic cats hugging on a shelf](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/hugging-1-1.jpg)

### Clinic Cats

_Masters of Mischief_

We have had an incredible run of GREAT clinic cats. It started with Vincent, a great ambassador who was with us for 6+ years. Vincent would sit in the front lobby and ‘allow’ all who entered to pet him. He left us in early 2016. A great loss for our team! April 2016 brought feline friends to Healthy Paws Animal Hospital, Gus Gus (orange longhaired) and Eleanor (gray shorthaired). They have learned the ropes of being clinic cats, sauntering around in spite of barking, vacuums, and, at times, mayhem. Be sure to pet our friendly, mischievous felines on your next visit!

![Gus cat](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Gus.jpg)

### Gus Gus

Gus Gus has a more regal presence and is ever the socialite, making sure everyone receives a proper welcome. Gus is down-to-earth and a hanging-out kind of guy. While he does not seek adults out, he has a special place in his heart for kids. And if you see a paw under your exam room door someday, it is his! Gus’ favorite pastimes are playing with his Turbo Scratcher toy, eating food from his SlimCat Toy, and carrying around his favorite mouse.

![Eleanor cat](https://healthypawsanimalhospital.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Eleanor-cat.jpg)

### Eleanor

Eleanor could be described as goofy and presumptuous. A real queen bee, she demands attention and solitude based on her mood and is known for “putting herself to bed” in a variety of cabinets, drawers, and rooms. And oh, the look if you interrupt her beauty sleep! Her favorite pastime is being in people’s business and sitting in laps.
