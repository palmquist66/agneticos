## Step 2: Add "Veterinarian" Category (5 min) — HIGH impact

Right now HP only has "Animal Hospital" as its category. More people search "veterinarian near me" than "animal hospital near me." Adding it as a secondary category means you show up for both.

**How to do it:**
1. Click **"Edit profile"**
2. Go to **"Business category"** (under About or Business Information)
3. Below "Animal Hospital," click **"Add another category"**
4. Type **"Veterinarian"** and select it from the dropdown
5. Click **Save**

Category changes can take 24-72 hours to go live — Google reviews them.

**Don't add "Emergency Veterinary Service"** unless HP actually offers emergency/after-hours care. Google suspends listings with misleading categories.

## Step 5: Review Responses — Get to 100% (1-3 hours to catch up, then 5 min/day)

HP has 158 reviews at 4.7 stars. That's solid. The gap is response rate: you're at roughly 30-40%. Winding Creek responds to every single one. Potential patients see that.

Google also tracks response rate as a ranking signal. Responding to reviews tells Google the business is active and engaged.

**How to respond:**
1. On your management panel, click **"Read reviews"**
2. Find reviews without a reply underneath them
3. Click **"Reply"**
4. Type your response and click **Submit**
5. Shows up within 10 minutes

### Positive Reviews (4-5 stars) — the majority of your backlog

Keep it 2-3 sentences. Thank them, mention something specific, invite them back.

> "Thank you, Sarah! We're so glad Max's dental cleaning went well and that our team made you both feel comfortable. We look forward to seeing you at his next visit!"

> "Thanks for the kind words, Mike! Buddy is such a great patient. We're happy the new puppy wellness plan has been helpful — see you at his next round of shots!"

> "We really appreciate you taking the time to share this. It means a lot to the whole team. Looking forward to seeing you and Luna again soon!"

### Negative Reviews (1-2 stars) — handle carefully

Don't get defensive. Don't share private pet/medical details publicly. Take it offline.

> "Thank you for sharing your feedback. We're sorry your experience didn't meet expectations and take all concerns seriously. Please reach out to us at (847) 854-2322 so we can discuss this directly."

### Neutral Reviews (3 stars)

Acknowledge what went well, briefly address the concern.

> "Thanks for the feedback. We're glad [positive part] and sorry about [concern]. We'd love to make your next visit even better — don't hesitate to let us know how we can help."

### Rules
- Respond within 24 hours of new reviews going forward
- Vary your responses — don't copy-paste the same reply every time
- Never argue publicly
- Keep it 2-5 sentences
- Mention the pet's name when the reviewer includes it — makes it personal
- Never share medical details, billing info, or anything private

### Catching up the backlog

Work through unanswered reviews oldest to newest. Replying to a year-old review is better than leaving it blank. Google doesn't penalize late responses.

> **New in 2026:** Google is testing AI-suggested review replies. If you see a suggestion button next to a review, it'll draft a reply you can edit. Always personalize it before submitting — the auto-generated ones are generic.
