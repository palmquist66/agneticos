### Pattern Insights

- **Primary category split:** HPAH and Cary Grove use "Animal hospital" while VCA, Dundee, and Pet Vet use "Veterinarian." Both are valid — "Animal hospital" signals full-service, "Veterinarian" captures more general searches. Having both covers all bases.
- **Zero competitors have secondaries.** Adding 3-4 secondary categories immediately gives HPAH the broadest search footprint in the market. Nobody else is doing this.
- **Category = search trigger.** Each secondary category opens a new set of map pack queries. Four new categories could mean dozens of new search triggers within a week.
