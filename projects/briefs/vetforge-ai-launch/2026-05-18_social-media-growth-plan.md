# VetForge AI — Social Media Growth Plan

## Context

VetForge AI has three social accounts live (LinkedIn, X, Instagram) with zero content beyond a GBP intro post. The primary sales channel is buyer group referrals, not social media directly — but when a buyer group intro happens, the prospect will Google "VetForge AI" and check socials. Empty profiles kill credibility. Social media's job is to make sure that when someone looks you up, they see an active, knowledgeable presence that confirms the peer recommendation.

**Time budget:** 3-5 hours/week
**Goal:** Build credibility + generate leads (both)
**Social proof:** HPAH results available but need anonymizing

---

## Platform Priority

**LinkedIn = primary (60% of effort)**
- Where vet practice owners, buyer group contacts, and decision-makers make B2B decisions
- When someone in the buyer group says "talk to James at VetForge AI," the first thing they'll do is check your LinkedIn
- Longest content shelf life — posts surface for days, not hours

**X = secondary (25% of effort)**
- GEO/SEO industry thought leadership — positions you as an expert in the emerging space
- Quick-hit commentary on AI trends, Google updates, vet industry news
- Good for connecting with other marketing professionals who might refer vet clients

**Instagram = tertiary (15% of effort)**
- Visual proof of work — screenshots of results, before/after, infographics
- Low-effort repurposed content from LinkedIn — don't create original content here
- Park it with consistent reposts; ramp up later when you have visual case studies

---

## Content Pillars (5 themes to rotate)

### 1. GEO Education (30% of posts)
What GEO is, why it matters for vets, how AI search is changing how pet owners find vets. This is your unique angle — nobody else in the vet marketing space is talking about this.

### 2. Anonymized Case Study Content (25% of posts)
Real results from your HPAH work, anonymized. Before/after metrics, specific wins, tangible outcomes. This is your social proof until you have multiple clients.

### 3. Pain Point Content (20% of posts)
Posts that make vet practice owners feel seen. These are the posts that get shared in buyer groups and Facebook groups because they describe the exact frustration.

### 4. Industry Commentary (15% of posts)
Trends in vet marketing, corporate chain moves, AI adoption news. Quick takes that show you're paying attention and thinking about the industry.

### 5. Behind the Scenes / Founder Story (10% of posts)
Building in public. What it's like building VetForge AI from inside a real practice. Personal, founder-led content that humanizes the brand.

## Weekly Cadence (3-5 hours total)

| Day | Activity | Time | Platform |
|-----|----------|------|----------|
| **Monday** | Write 1 anchor LinkedIn post (long-form, 150-250 words) | 45 min | LinkedIn |
| **Monday** | Repurpose anchor into X thread or post + Instagram caption | 20 min | X, Instagram |
| **Wednesday** | Write 1 quick-hit post (pain point, industry take, or GEO tip) | 30 min | LinkedIn + X |
| **Friday** | Write 1 case study or behind-the-scenes post | 45 min | LinkedIn |
| **Friday** | Repurpose Friday post for X + Instagram | 15 min | X, Instagram |
| **Daily** | 10-15 min engagement (reply to comments, comment on others' posts) | 70 min/week | LinkedIn + X |

**Weekly output:** 3 LinkedIn posts, 3-4 X posts, 2 Instagram posts
**Total time:** ~3.5-4 hours/week

## Content Creation Workflow

1. **Write the anchor piece** — draft in VetForge AI voice (voice-profile.md loaded)
2. **Run through humanizer** — `tool-humanizer` in deep mode before posting
3. **Repurpose across platforms** — adapt voice per platform (LinkedIn = authority, X = punchy, IG = visual-first)
4. **Save to project folder** — `projects/briefs/vetforge-ai-launch/social-content/` with date-prefixed files

Each week's content gets saved as:

## Engagement Strategy (10-15 min/day)

**LinkedIn:**
- Comment on posts from vet industry people, practice management consultants, buyer group contacts
- Engage in vet practice owner groups
- Reply to every comment on your posts within 24 hours

**X:**
- Follow and engage with GEO/SEO thought leaders
- Comment on Google algorithm update threads
- Reply to vet industry conversations

**Don't do:**
- Follow-for-follow or like-for-like
- Generic motivational content
- Hashtag spam (1-3 relevant on LinkedIn, 0-1 on X)
