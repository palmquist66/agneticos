# LinkedIn Post 1 — Monday Anchor
**Pillar:** GEO Education
**Post date:** May 19, 2026

---

I asked ChatGPT to recommend a veterinarian in a mid-size Illinois town.

It named three practices. Two were corporate chains. The independent animal hospital down the street, the one with 20 years of community trust, wasn't mentioned.

Pet owners are skipping Google and asking AI instead. ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google's own AI Overview. They're all answering "who's the best vet near me?" And they're pulling from signals most independent practices aren't sending.

What kind of signals? Recent activity on your Google Business Profile. Services and staff listed on your website in a way AI can actually read. Review volume, not just your star rating. Whether you show up in local directories.

The corporate chains have people handling all of that. Your practice probably hasn't touched its Google listing in months.

That gap gets wider every week.

Here's what I keep telling practice owners: AI doesn't care how big you are. It cares whether your online presence gives it something to work with. A 10-person independent clinic can show up ahead of a corporate chain if the right pieces are in place.

Most of them aren't hard to fix. They're just not getting done because nobody has the time.

If that sounds like your practice, that's exactly what I work on.
