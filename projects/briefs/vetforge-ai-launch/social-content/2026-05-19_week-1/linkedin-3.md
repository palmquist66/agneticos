# LinkedIn Post 3 — Friday Anchor
**Pillar:** Founder Story / Behind the Scenes
**Post date:** May 23, 2026

---

I built VetForge AI because I watched it happen from the inside.

My wife works at an independent animal hospital. Solid vets, loyal staff, the kind of place where they know every patient by name and nobody leaves on time when an emergency walks in at 5:45.

But the business side was barely holding together.

Their Google listing hadn't been updated in over a year. Social media was sporadic. When I searched for a vet in their area, the corporate chains came up first, even though this practice had been serving the community for two decades.

So I started fixing things. Not as a consultant who shows up with a slide deck and leaves. From the inside, sitting in the office, learning the actual workflow, seeing what gets in the way of everything else.

I audited their Google Business Profile. Found missing categories, no service descriptions, zero posts. Turns out nobody in the local market was doing this either, but that just meant there was an opening.

Then I set up AI to handle the content, review responses, and posting schedule. All the stuff that was falling through the cracks because there weren't enough hours in the day.

That's how VetForge AI started. Inside a real animal hospital. Not a pitch deck.
